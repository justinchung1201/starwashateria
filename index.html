Good to know

Frequently asked questions

What are your hours?

We're open 7 days a week, 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM.

What size washers and dryers do you have?

Brand-new, high-efficiency Speed Queen washers and dryers in every size — from small everyday loads up to large machines that easily handle comforters and bulky bedding.

How does pricing work?

Self-service machines are priced by capacity; wash & fold and pickup & delivery are priced by the pound. Call (346) 440-9180 for today's exact rates — and don't forget our weekly specials.

Do you offer pickup & delivery? What area do you cover?

Yes — free pickup and delivery across the Houston area. Schedule online and we'll collect, wash, fold, and return your laundry on your schedule.

What payment methods do you accept?

Our machines accept both coin and coinless (card) payment, so you can pay however is most convenient.

What's available while I wait?

Fresh A/C, free high-speed WiFi, free TV, a comfortable lounge, on-site laundry supplies, and friendly, bilingual staff.
